How to use TaskBoard

  • Add a box:
  • Move a box: Drag it anywhere on the canvas. Position is saved automatically.
  • Delete a box: — or use the ✕ button that appears on hover.
  • Switch boards: Tap a board tab at the top.
  • Rename a board: Click the ✎ icon next to a tab, or use the Manage tab.
  • Scroll: If boxes are placed beyond the visible area, scroll bars appear automatically.
  • Collaborate: Changes from other devices appear in real time via WebSocket, with 1-second polling as fallback.
